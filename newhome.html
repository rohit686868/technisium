Siddaganga Institute of Technology

Technisium is a national-level student's technical symposium being organized annually under the aegis of the Association of Electrical Sciences which comprises departments of ECE, EEE, E&I, and ETE of Siddaganga Institute of Technology. Technisium was started in the year 1996 and this Technisium-22 is the 25th in this series.
